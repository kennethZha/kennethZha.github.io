Art75

The two contrasting videos will be on opposite sides of the space, so you would look at one video at a time with the other behind you. The videos will be displayed behind mirrors, so the audience will see their reflections in the videos. This sparks the question of "balance" between nature and progress and makes you contemplate your own balance. The room is split unevenly into black and white as to contrast the balance in our society between nature and progress.
